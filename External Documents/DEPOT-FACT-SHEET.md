## Sort model — SSD (important)

Woodland LCD 1 / LCD 2 are **SSD depots (Separate Sort from Delivery)**:

- A **router** sorts the manual mail during the day.
- The **delivery agent** (the carrier) delivers it the **next day** and does **NOT** case or tie out
  their own mail.
- DA packet-prep flow uses **C-52 / C-48**.
- Missorts / RTS handled via **C-44** and **POCUR**. (Tie-out is a marked fallback only.)
- SSD loading rule: **Stop & Go LFT in the driver's compartment**.
- The carrier may sometimes be **called in as a router** instead of delivering — that's a different
  workflow.

**Open question:** it is not fully confirmed whether *all* Woodland routes are SSD or it varies.
Assume SSD unless the route header says otherwise, and flag the difference when it matters.

## LCD 1 vs LCD 2 — delivery profiles

**LCD 1 — core downtown & West End.** West End, Yaletown, Coal Harbour, central Downtown business
district (Georgia, Robson, Granville). High-rises, commercial towers, dense residential. Lots of
apartment panels, concierge drop-offs, corporate mailrooms, high parcel volume. Expect a lot of
indoor time with **master keys and buzzers**.

**LCD 2 — historic core & east downtown.** Gastown, Chinatown, the **Downtown Eastside (DTES)**,
parts of Strathcona / False Creek Flats depending on route splits. Historic character buildings,
**single-room occupancy (SRO) hotels**, social housing, independent storefronts, light industrial.
Requires high situational awareness, street-level walking, secure mail slots, community mailrooms.
